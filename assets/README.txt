Homemade Stories — asset folder
================================

Drop the following files into this folder:

1. logo.png
   The illustrated logo (the image used in the hero). If the file is
   missing the hero will automatically fall back to a placeholder card
   reminding you to add it.

   Recommended: PNG with transparency, around 880×1200 px
   (the hero displays it up to 440px wide on desktop and scales down).

Optional:

2. app-store-badge.svg
   Apple's official "Download on the App Store" badge if you'd prefer
   it over the styled text button currently in use. You can download
   it from Apple's marketing resources:
   https://tools.applemediaservices.com/app-store/
   To use it, replace the <a class="btn btn-primary"> blocks in
   index.html with an <img src="assets/app-store-badge.svg" alt="...">.

Before publishing, also remember to:
 - Replace APP_STORE_URL in index.html (appears twice) with the real
   App Store product link once your app is live.
 - Replace the support email support@homemade-stories.app in
   support.html and privacy.html with your real contact address.
 - Replace the template privacy policy copy in privacy.html with a
   real policy reviewed for your jurisdiction (especially important
   for children's apps — COPPA, GDPR-K, Apple Kids Category rules).
